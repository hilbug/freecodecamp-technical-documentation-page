Dawson's Creek

Character Bios

Josephine Potter

Josephine Potter, known as Joey, is a childhood friend of Dawson Leery. She lives with her sister, sister's boyfriend, and nephew. Her mother died of cancer when she was young. Her father is in prison for trafficking drugs. She is one of the smartest students at Capeside High and hopes to attend Wentworth upon graduation.

Dawson Leery

Dawson Leery is a childhood friend of Joey Potter and Pacey Witter. He lives with his mother and father right on the creek. His a film buff, his favorite director being Stephen Spielberg. He hopes to move to L.A. to attend film school. It was love at first sight when Jen Lindley stepped out of that cab from New York City.

Pacey Witter

Pacey Witter is the childhood friend of Dawson Leery. They also work at a video rental store together. Pacey caused quite the controversy when rumors swirled about relationship with a teacher, Miss Jacobs. He lives at home with his parents and siblings and doesn't have the greatest relationship with his father and older brother.

Jennifer Lindley

Jennifer Lindley, known as Jen, is the new next door neighbor of Dawson Leery from New York City. She was sent to live with her grandmother after getting into trouble with boys, drugs, and alcohol. She was Dawson Leery's first girlfriend but ends up breaking his heart. She later becomes best friends with Jack McPhee.
